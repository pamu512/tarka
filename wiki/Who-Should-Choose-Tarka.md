# Who Should Choose Tarka

Tarka is best for teams that need fraud decisioning they can customize deeply and operate with confidence.

## Who Should Use Tarka

Use Tarka if you need to combine prevention, detection, and investigation in one auditable workflow rather than operating separate point tools.

## Best Fit Teams

- Product and risk teams at fintechs, payment processors, neobanks, lenders, exchanges, and marketplaces.
- Teams that need both real-time controls and post-decision investigation workflows.
- Organizations with strict audit/compliance expectations that require traceable evidence and control visibility.
- Engineering teams that prefer open-source extensibility over black-box vendor logic.
- Companies that need to connect multiple providers (KYC, sanctions, device, IP, phone, dispute, CRM) behind one platform.

## Common Reasons Teams Pick Tarka

- Unified stack: rules, ML, graph signals, case ops, and integration management.
- Modular deployment: run lite, full stack, or only selected services.
- Strong operability: health endpoints, rotation jobs, vault/KMS support, and runbooks.
- Build-for-change model: no-code rule editing plus API-first automation.

## When Tarka May Be Too Much

If your use case is only a lightweight allow/deny check with no investigations, no integrations, and no governance requirements, a smaller point solution may be faster to start.

## Evaluation Tips

- Start with the sandbox quickstart.
- Enable 2-3 integrations you already use.
- Run simulation and compare with current outcomes.
- Review evidence exports and workflow fit with compliance and operations stakeholders.
