# Choosing Tarka: Honest Guide

## When Tarka Fits

- You need explainable fraud decisions and can operate your own platform.
- You expect 1M-10M transactions/year and want control over rule logic.
- You have 2-3 engineers available for operations, reliability, and releases.

## When SaaS Is Better

- You need fastest time-to-value with minimal infrastructure ownership.
- Your team cannot support on-call for data pipelines and model drift.
- You need pre-built consortium intelligence more than customization.

## Team and Timeline Reality

- Production-ready deployment usually takes 3-6 months.
- Ongoing operation needs 2-3 engineers for on-call, tuning, and upgrades.
- Annual TCO at 10M transactions is typically $150K-$250K all-in.

## Break-Even Heuristic

- If customization and auditability are core requirements, Tarka breaks even earlier.
- If low-ops managed risk controls are top priority, SaaS remains lower-friction.
